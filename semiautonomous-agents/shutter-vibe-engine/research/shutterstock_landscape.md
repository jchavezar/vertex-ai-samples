| --------------- | ---------- |

## Scale

* ~200 M royalty-free photos / vectors / illustrations
* ~10 M video clips on the core platform; 44 M+ at Pond5 (Jan 2025)
* 1.4 M+ 3D models on TurboSquid
* Giphy: 1.7 B daily users, ~1 B GIFs/day
* 100 K+ contributors across the family of brands
* 2025 revenue: $989.9 M (record), +6 % YoY; Adjusted EBITDA $271.8 M
* Combined with Getty (merger DOJ-cleared Feb 2026): 600 M+ assets, ~$2 B revenue

## AI initiatives

* OpenAI partnership — six-year data licensing (DALL-E training data; powers Shutterstock.AI)
* Meta, LG AI Research, Apple, Reka, NVIDIA — additional licensing deals
* Contributor Fund — bi-annual payouts; ~$4.24 M in May 2023
* Generative tools live inside Creative Flow
* Legacy CNN-based vision: Spectrum (color), Palette, Reverse Image Search — first-gen, ripe for replacement

## Discovery weaknesses (Gemini embeddings can fix all of these)

1. Synonym / paraphrase fragility ("cozy" vs "warm" vs "intimate")
2. Intent / abstraction ("the calm before a storm")
3. Multilingual nuance loss (English-biased catalog metadata)
4. Single-modality "more like this" (no photo → music jump)
5. No style / mood transfer search
6. Cross-catalog blindness (Pond5 / TurboSquid / Giphy / Envato siloed indices)
7. Editorial moment matching is keyword-guess work
8. Manual brief decomposition by users
9. Rule-based brand-safety with no per-customer policy
10. Long-tail unfindability driven by inconsistent contributor tagging

## Personas

| Persona | Pain | What they'd love |
| ------- | ---- | ---------------- |
| Agency creative director | Hours of keyword fishing for mood boards | Brief → curated visual sets in seconds |
| Brand designer (F500) | Cohesion across a campaign | Logo / brand-guide → on-style assets |
| Social-media manager | Reactive to news, needs many on-trend assets | Editorial moment finder; trending clusters |
| Enterprise media buyer | Cross-modal multilingual briefs | One vector space across photo / video / 3D / music |
| API developer | Keyword-only ID lookups | Embedding endpoint to drop into vector DB |
| News publisher | Right photo for breaking story in minutes | Article-text → photo retrieval |
| AI / data-licensing customer | Wants curated training subsets by concept | Embedding clustering and slicing |
| Pond5 / TurboSquid contributor | Hard-to-tag work going undiscovered | Auto-tagging + auto-categorization at upload |

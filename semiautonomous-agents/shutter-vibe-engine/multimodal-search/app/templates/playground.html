📖 Vertex Vector Search Core Concepts
Approximate Nearest Neighbors (ANN) & ScaNN
Standard databases index exact keywords. Vector databases map conceptual distances. Using the **ScaNN (Sparsified Coordinate Anisotropic Nearest Neighbors)** tree-clustering algorithm, Google clusters vectors into hierarchies, scanning less than 1% of data to locate matches in under 10ms.
Cosine Distance Metric
The index calculates proximity using the angle between vectors: $$\text{similarity} = 1.0 - \text{distance}$$ A distance close to $0.0$ represents pointing in the same direction (strong conceptual match), whereas $2.0$ represents complete opposites.
Dynamic Namespace Restrictions
Rather than scanning all vectors and then filtering results afterward (which causes leakage and misses), Vertex AI executes **Boolean Filtering** during the ScaNN graph traversal itself. Matches must satisfy namespaces (`env`, `modality`) as they are calculated.
